About me!

Hello there, everyone! Rafael Jimenez is my name, and I am currently a software engineering major. This is my second semester at this university. I came from the University of Maryland and transferred here. I'm still getting used to living in a tiny town. Honestly not sure if I can ever adjust but looking forward to the future.

I never imagined I'd wind up in Idaho, especially since I'm not a church member. It's amusing to consider now that I'm here. I transferred from a large party school to a small one, which helped me turn my life around. It was a Sunday when I initially arrived. I went to get some lunch because I was hungry, but nothing was open. I was unhappy, but it has helped me get back in shape now that time has passed.

The most striking thing I've observed is how nice everyone is. Because I grew up in a difficult neighborhood, I occasionally cry about it. Kindness aids in the development of the heart!

Finally!!!

I got accepted to my dream school the University of Miami! Still not sure what to do, but I know with prayer I will figure it out.
